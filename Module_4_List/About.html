Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a non-stick pan, add chopped ginger and garlic. Sauté 1 minute. Add chopped onion and curry leaves, sauté until golden brown. Add minced beef, stir 2 to 3 minutes. Add all the spices and stir next 2 minutes. Add salt as required, cover and cook for 5 minutes in a lower heat. ( Do not add water, the cooking beef produce sufficient water) Uncover the pan and cook until the beef mix very dry in texture. Remove from heat and leave it to cool. When it’s cool, add mashed potato and 2 tablespoon bread crumbs. Add salt if needed, combine well. Beef cutlet mix is ready. Take a small portion of cutlet mix in your palm; make a small flat round shape. Dip it into beaten egg white. When egg whites well coated, place it into bread crumbs and cover/roll well.keep aside.
